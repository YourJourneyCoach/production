The Enneagram

A profound framework for self-discovery, leadership development, and optimizing team dynamics.

A ROADMAP TO AWARENESS

What is the Enneagram?

The Enneagram is an ancient, highly nuanced framework that describes nine distinct personality archetypes. Unlike traditional personality tests that focus on what you do, the Enneagram reveals why you do it. By uncovering your core motivations, fears, and internal operating systems, it provides a highly accurate roadmap for personal growth and leadership development.

Advantages for Individuals

  • Uncover Blind Spots: Recognize unconscious patterns and reactive behaviors that limit your leadership effectiveness.
  • Accelerated Self-Awareness: Cultivate a profound understanding of your strengths and specific areas for development.
  • Emotional Intelligence: Learn how to self-regulate under pressure and make intentional, rather than reactive, decisions.

Advantages for Teams

  • Enhanced Communication: Radically improve how team members speak and listen to each other by understanding different cognitive processing styles.
  • Conflict Resolution: De-escalate tension by recognizing that conflict often stems from differing, valid world views rather than malice.
  • Cohesion & Trust: Build a foundation of psychological safety where individuals feel seen, understood, and valued for their unique contributions.
